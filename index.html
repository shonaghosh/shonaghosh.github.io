Shona Ghosh

Senior Tech Editor, Insider Inc

London, UK

Shona Ghosh is a Senior Technology Editor for the business section of Insider, based in London. Read more...

She oversees the UK tech team's coverage, running both day-to-day news and in-depth investigations. In 2019, she was awarded Tech Journalist of the Year at the UK Tech Awards.

She has been a tech journalist for a decade, and has been published in The Guardian, The Telegraph, Al Jazeera, and NME.com. Before Insider, she was Technology Editor at UK advertising trade publication Campaign.

In her capacity as Senior Tech Editor, and as a senior reporter before that, she has written investigative features on firms including Amazon, WeWork, Deliveroo, and Snapchat.

She appears regularly on the BBC and Sky News to talk about breaking technology news, trends, and business.
