Shona Ghosh

Senior Editor, Bloomberg

London, UK

Shona Ghosh is a UK-based senior editor at Bloomberg. Read more...

She joined Bloomberg's technology, media, and telecoms team in Europe in February 2024, helping oversee breaking news and enterprise reporting.

She has been a tech journalist for a decade, and has been published in The Guardian, The Telegraph, Al Jazeera, and NME.com. Before Bloomberg, she was UK Deputy Executive Editor at Business Insider. Across her career, she has written investigative features on firms including Amazon, WeWork, Deliveroo, and Snapchat.

She won Technology Journalist of the Year at the 2019 UK Tech Awards, led Business Insider's venture capital and startups team to best specialist/B2B website at the Press Gazette Future of Media Awards in 2023, and was part of the Bloomberg reporting team that won the Newspaper or Digital Breaking News Reporting category at the Deadline Club Awards 2025.

She appears regularly on the BBC and Sky News to talk about breaking technology news, trends, and business.
